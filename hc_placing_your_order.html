Placing Your Order

We only display items that are in stock and available to be shipped (except when shipped from the manufacturer). If it's not on the site, it's not currently in stock. We can't accept orders for item s that are out of stock.

All available sizes for a particular item are displayed on the product detail page. If the item is no longer available in a certain size, that size will be grayed out. We are constantly adding new pro ducts daily and work hard to ensure our website has the most updated inventory. Please continue to review our online catalog for any updates.

We currently accept online payments with a number of payment methods. A full list of available payment methods will be displayed at the checkout when placing your order.

We reserve the right to withhold any order from despatch to process further security checks. This can delay an order for up to 36 hours. If your offer to purchase goods is refused, any money p aid by you will be refunded.

How do I change the currency on the site?

Click the appropriate currency symbol at the top right of the page. When you click one of these symbols, the page you are on will refresh and all prices will be displayed in your new currenc y. Prices will be displayed in your new currency until you change it again, even if you leave the site and come back later.

Can I change my order?

If you wish to change your order, you will need to contact the Customer Services team as soon as possible after placing your order. This is because we start processing the order as soon as the order confirmation email is sent. This enables us to provide you with the quickest possible service.

For orders shipping to the EU, any relevant duties & taxes will be displayed and charged at the checkout. For orders shipping to other countries, any import duties, or similar charges due on you r order will be levied once the package reaches its destination country. Customs policies vary widely from country to country, and we advise you to contact your local customs office for further information.
