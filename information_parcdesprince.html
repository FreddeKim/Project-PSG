The Parc des Princes is a legendary venue that football fans from all over the world dream of visiting! Take a look at the stadium map ahead of your visit to locate your nearest entrance and the services that might be of interest to you.

The tension rises with just a few minutes to go before kickoff. Make sure you're in the stands and don't miss any of the action from Paris Saint-Germain.

  • Paris stand : Doors A, B, C, D, PMR, E, F, G and visitor
  • Auteuil stand : Doors H and I
  • Borelli stand : Doors J, K, L and M
  • Boulogne stand : Doors N, O and P

From your place to the stadium, our interactive tool is here to help you!

It has never been easier to get to the Parc des Princes! Whether you are travelling from Paris itself or the outskirts, here you will find plenty of tips for getting to Paris Saint-Germain’s stadium quickly and easily. Check them out now to ensure you don’t miss a second of the match, but don’t forget: on match days, it can be tricky getting around the outside of the stadium due to the various security barriers in place.

By Metro: Line 9: Porte de Saint-Cloud stop. Line 10: Porte d'Auteuil stop.
By bus: Lines 22, 62, 72: Porte de Saint-Cloud stop. Lines 32 and 52: Porte d'Auteuil stop.
By car: From the South ring road: exit D910 / Boulogne.
